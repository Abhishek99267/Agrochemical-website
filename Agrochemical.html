What is agrochemical ?

Agrochemicals, or agricultural chemicals, are chemical products are used to control weeds, insects, rodents, fungus, and other harmful organisms and diseases of crops, vegetables, fruits, and other agriculture to control pests, pathogens, and supply nutrients to the soil. They include: 

•Pesticides, such as insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, and nematicides
•Synthetic fertilizers
•Hormones and other chemical growth agents
•Concentrated stores of raw animal manure
•Fuels
•Solvents
•Veterinary chemicals
