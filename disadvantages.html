DISADVANTAGES WHEN WE USE AGROCHEMICALS

 


•	Soil degradation  :
    Excessive use of fertilizers can contaminate groundwater with nitrate, which is harmful to humans and animals. It can also decrease the natural microflora in the soil.


•	Air pollution      :
    Agrochemicals sprayed in agricultural fields can drift away and pollute the air.


•	Biodiversity      :
    Agrochemicals can affect the growth of pests and decrease nonpest populations of insects and soil dwelling microbes. 


•	Bioaccumulation    :
    Agrochemicals can seep into the surrounding land and water bodies, entering the food chain. 


•	Nutrient imbalance :
    Excessive use of agrochemicals can generate a significant amount of residues that cause nutrient imbalance. 


•	Quality reduction  :
    Excessive use of agrochemicals can reduce the quality of agricultural produce. 


•	Human health       :
    Consumption of agrochemical residues has been linked to various illnesses, such as asthma.
